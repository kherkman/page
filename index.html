GAMES

3D Shooter

A first-person shooter game in a browser. Test your reflexes and aim in this retro-inspired game. Works best with a computer. Gamepad controls are enabled.

The Planet Commander

Strategize and conquer in this space-themed strategy game. Command your forces and expand your empire across the planet. You can design your solar system. Works best with a computer.

Grim Reaper Game

A 2D game where you embody the Grim Reaper in random generated levels. Collect the escaped ghosts. Developed for touch screen and computer.

Neon Pinball

Build your pinball in a browser. Works with a touch screen or a computer.

Space Fighter Jets

Classic 2D space shooter in a browser. 1 or 2 players. Works with a touch screen or a computer. Gamepad controls are enabled.

Bot Game

2D level jumping game with random generated levels. Collect the batteries for the bot. Developed for touch screen and computer.

Bunny Game

An adorable two player platformer featuring a cute bunny. Collect carrots in a charming random generated map. Developed for touch screen and computer.

Chaze

A unique blend of chess, changing labyrinth and rock, paper and scissors. Developed for touch screen and computer.

Hyper Cat

Hyper Cat must find a way to the goal in the hyperworld made of cubes and hypercubes. Developed for computer only. Finnish texts.

Chord Invaders

A musical shoot-em-up game where you defend against invading aliens with playing chords. Played with a midi keyboard.

Civilization Builder

2D RTS civilization building game with some puzzle elements. One player. Map generator. Designed for a computer but touch screen enabled.

MUSIC

Audio Player

Load your folder with mp3 or wav audio files and cover image as jpg and lyrics as txt to a playlist. If a jpg or txt with the song name is not found folder.jpg and lyrics.txt from the same folder is used.

Simple DAW

A simple digital audio workstation in web. Mix your audio files and export to a wav. Set up volume, pan, EQ and delay to each track. Trim and fade tracks. Some buttons are in Finnish.

Mastering Audio

A mastering studio in web. Master your audio file and export to a wav. Load a reference file. Set up LPF, HPF, dynamic EQ and Limiter. See LUFS-I and True Peaks. Info in Finnish.

Sheet Music Display

View and create sheet music compositions with microtonal accessibility. Midi improt and export inbluded. A handy tool for musicians and learners.

Guitar Fretboard With Scales and Chords

Display scales and chords. Microtonal tunings included. Create and edit guitar tablatures. A simple interface for guitarists, bassists etc..

Song Drum Machine

Build your own beats and rhythms with this interactive drum machine. Generate random drums for songs. Perfect for aspiring producers and beatmakers.

Musical Tuning Comparison

Explore different musical tunings and their unique characteristics. Understand the nuances of various temperaments.

Chromatic Circle

Visualize musical relationships with an interactive chromatic circle. Analyze any EDO. A helpful tool for music theory students.

Tunable Keys

Experiment with different key tunings and their sonic impact. Dive deep into the world of musical intonation.

Piano Scales Autoplay

Learn and practice piano scales with an autoplay feature. Perfect for beginners and those looking to refine their knowledge.

Dual/Multi Tuning Pianos

Explore pianos with multiple tuning systems simultaneously. A fascinating tool for advanced musical experimentation.

Retro Arcade Sequencer

Create chiptune and retro-style music with this arcade-inspired sequencer. Bring back the sounds of classic gaming.

Synthwave Lab

Generate captivating synth sounds and study wave shape addition. A modular synth in a browser.

Polyrhythm Sequencer

Generate rhythms seamlessly and export them as midi or wav.

Table Sequencer

Generate tunes in an table calculation program and export them as midi or wav.

Circle Tunes

Generate tunes seamlessly in an chromatic circle.
